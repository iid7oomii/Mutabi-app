## About the Project

**Mutabi** is an integrated digital platform designed to support children diagnosed with **Sensory Processing Disorder (SPD)** by bridging the gap between clinical therapy sessions and home-based exercise routines.

The platform enables therapists and specialists to create structured sensory therapy plans that families can follow at home, while also giving specialists remote visibility into how well patients are adhering to their programs. Mutabi can be adopted by hospitals and clinics, with a customizable visual identity for each healthcare provider.

---

## Problem Statement

Many families with children diagnosed with SPD face difficulties consistently following therapeutic exercises at home. Even when specialists design appropriate therapy plans, parents may struggle to:

- Fully understand how to perform the exercises correctly
- Track their child's progress over time
- Stay aligned with evolving therapy plans

At the same time, specialists lack an effective system to monitor whether patients are completing home exercises between sessions — making it hard to evaluate real progress or adjust treatment plans accordingly.

**Mutabi solves this by creating a unified monitoring system that connects specialists with families**, enabling continuous follow-up and improving the overall effectiveness of therapy programs.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 📋 **Therapy Plan Management** | Specialists create structured therapy plans with exercises and activities |
| 🏠 **Home Exercise Guidance** | Parents receive clear, step-by-step instructions to perform exercises with their child |
| 📈 **Progress Tracking** | Families log completed activities and monitor the child's development over time |
| 🔍 **Specialist Monitoring** | Remote access for specialists to review progress and adjust therapy plans |
| 💬 **Communication Support** | Built-in tools to strengthen communication between families and healthcare providers |
| 🏥 **Multi-Clinic Support** | Customizable branding for hospitals and clinics that adopt the platform |

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved consistency of sensory exercises performed at home
- Stronger communication between specialists and families
- Better data-driven insights into each child's progress
- Higher overall effectiveness of therapy programs through continuous follow-up
- Scalability to support multiple hospitals and clinics
