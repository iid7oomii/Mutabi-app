## 0. Project Objectives
 
### Purpose
The project aims to bridge the digital gap in Occupational Therapy through an integrated platform that connects specialists via a Web Dashboard with children's parents through a Mobile App, to enable precise and interactive monitoring of sensory exercises, ensuring the continuity and efficiency of children's exercises outside the clinic.
 
### SMART Objectives
1. **Operational Efficiency:** Reducing the burden and pressure on specialists and lowering the clinic's operational costs by automating remote routine exercise monitoring. This allows the clinic to increase its capacity and accommodate a larger number of patients.
2. **Treatment Plan Management:** Providing specialists with interactive tools via a Web Dashboard, enabling them to easily build, customize, and assign Treatment Plans to patients.
3. **Clinical Focus:** Enabling specialists to dedicate their time and effort in the clinic to complex cases requiring close examination and direct intervention, while stable cases and daily exercises are monitored through the system.

## 1. Stakeholders and Team Roles
 
### Stakeholders
 
1. **Development Team:** The core group responsible for the end-to-end design, development, and successful delivery of the system.
2. **Clinics & Rehab Centers:** The institutional beneficiaries leveraging the system to reduce administrative overhead and expand patient capacity.
3. **Occupational Therapists:** The primary users of the Web Dashboard, tasked with managing treatment plans and monitoring clinical progress.
4. **Parents / Caregivers:** The End Users of the Mobile App, actively engaged in facilitating and tracking the daily exercises at home.

## 2. Project Scope
 
### In-Scope
 
- Developing a Web Dashboard for specialists to create treatment plans and track patient progress.
- Developing a Mobile App for parents to view assigned exercises and log their daily completion.
- Implementing a basic Authentication system for login and account creation.
 
### Out-of-Scope
 
- In-app direct Video Conferencing / Telehealth.
- Integration with Payment Gateways.
- Automated Diagnosis tools – the system is designed for tracking and facilitating tasks, not for medical diagnosis.
- In-App Messaging / Live Chat between parents and specialists.

## 3. Risk Management
 
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|:---:|:---:|---|
| Timeline Constraints: Insufficient time for MVP component integration. | High | High | Utilize Agile Sprints to prioritize core MVP functionality and defer non-essential features. |
| Resource Availability: Individual task delays creating team blockers. | Medium | High | Conduct Daily Stand-ups and utilize tracking tools like Trello to identify and resolve blockers early. |
| Learning Curve: Taking too much time to learn the required tools & frameworks. | High | Medium | Apply Timeboxing for learning and avoid diving into unnecessary details for the MVP. Rely on official Documentation and build small Prototypes first. |
| Technical Dependencies: Challenges in the Integration between the mobile app, web dashboard, and Database management. | Medium | High | Clearly plan and design the System Architecture and database schema before coding. Conduct Continuous Testing for API Endpoints early to avoid issues later. |
